When to go

Cheapest Time to Fly to Miami

Miami fares rise sharply in winter when northern travelers escape the cold, and drop in the hotter, wetter months.

☀️

Cheapest months

Late spring through early fall (May to September) brings the lowest fares, as it is hotter and is the rainy and hurricane season.

❄️

Winter is peak

December through March is high season when snowbirds arrive. Expect the highest fares and hotel rates around the holidays.

📈

Most expensive

Christmas, New Year, spring break, and major events like Art Basel and Miami Music Week spike prices. Book early for those.

📅

Best booking window

Book domestic Miami flights about 1 to 3 months ahead. For winter peak travel, aim for 2 to 4 months out, and set a price alert.
