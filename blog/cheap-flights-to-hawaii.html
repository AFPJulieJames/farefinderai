Cheap Flights to Hawaii

JJ
Julie James
Founder, FareFinderAI

TL;DR

Fly into Honolulu (HNL) for the lowest Hawaii fares and most flights - then hop to other islands if needed. The cheapest months are shoulder season: roughly late April to early June and September to early December (outside holidays). Depart from a West Coast gateway (LAX, SFO, SEA, San Diego) where transpacific fares are lowest, fly midweek, and set price alerts. If you're not on the coast, a positioning flight to the West Coast can still net big savings.

Hawaii feels like a splurge, but the airfare is very beatable if you get three things right: the island you fly into, the season, and your departure city. Here's how to do all three.

Which island airport is cheapest?

Honolulu (HNL) on Oahu is almost always the cheapest and best-served gateway. Direct fares to Maui (OGG), Kona (KOA), and Kauai (LIH) typically run higher, so flying into Honolulu and taking a short, cheap inter-island hop often beats a direct flight to the outer islands.

The cheapest months to fly

Shoulder season wins: roughly late April to early June and September to early December (skip Thanksgiving). These windows pair low fares with great weather and thinner crowds. The pricey stretches are summer, Christmas/New Year, and spring break. Stack your dates with the cheapest days to fly (midweek) for more savings.

Depart from the West Coast

Transpacific fares are lowest from West Coast gateways - LAX, SFO, SEA, San Diego, sometimes Las Vegas and Phoenix. If you live elsewhere, compare a one-stop via the coast, and consider a separate cheap positioning flight to a coast hub, then the Hawaii fare from there. The savings on the long transpacific leg often dwarf the positioning cost.

More ways to save

  • Set alerts - Hawaii sees frequent fare sales; pounce when one drops.
  • Be flexible on island and let the cheapest gateway pick your base.
  • Bundle flight + hotel/condo - packages often beat booking separately for resort stays.
  • Mix airlines on outbound and return if a one-way combo is cheaper.

Frequently asked questions

What is the cheapest time to fly to Hawaii?
Shoulder season - late April to early June and September to early December, outside holidays. Avoid summer, Christmas, and spring break.
Which Hawaii airport is cheapest to fly into?
Honolulu (HNL) almost always has the lowest fares and most flights. Flying into HNL and hopping to other islands often beats a direct outer-island fare.
How can I save on flights to Hawaii?
Depart a West Coast gateway, fly midweek in shoulder season, set price alerts, and consider a positioning flight to the coast if you live elsewhere.

Honolulu, shoulder season, West Coast departure - get those three right and Hawaii is far cheaper than its reputation.
