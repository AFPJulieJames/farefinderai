About FareFinderAI

Helping travelers stop overpaying for flights - free, with no signup.

FareFinderAI exists for one reason: most people overpay for flights, and they don't have to. Airlines and booking sites count on travelers checking a single search engine, accepting the first fare they see, and never learning the strategies that frequent flyers use to pay far less. We're here to close that gap.

Who's behind it

FareFinderAI was founded by Julie James, who got tired of watching friends and family pay hundreds more than they needed to for the same seats. What started as sharing fare-hunting tips turned into a full resource: testing every major flight-search tool, documenting the strategies that actually work, and building a free site that puts them all in one place.

Julie runs FareFinderAI under Automated Income Tools LLC. Every guide and review on the site reflects hands-on testing of the tools we recommend - not rephrased press releases.

How we test and what we recommend

We compare flight-search tools the way a careful traveler would: running the same routes across multiple engines, checking which one surfaces the lowest fare, and noting the catches (bag fees, basic-economy restrictions, which budget carriers get left out). When we say Aviasales is our top pick for fare search or Kiwi is best for hidden-city routes, it's because we ran the searches. See our tool comparison and Aviasales review for the full breakdowns.

We also write honestly about the risks of aggressive strategies. Our hidden city ticketing guide and skiplagging legality guide lay out exactly when these tactics save money and when they can backfire - because telling only half the story doesn't help anyone.

FareFinderAI is free because of affiliate partnerships. When you book or sign up through some of our links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you - you pay the same price you would anyway. This never changes which tools we recommend; our rankings are based on testing, not commission rates. Full details are in our affiliate disclosure.
