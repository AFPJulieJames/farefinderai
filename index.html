How to Find Cheaper Flights Every Time

The strategies frequent travelers use to pay less on every booking - wherever they fly.

Tip 01

Search in Incognito Mode

Prices can creep up when sites detect repeat searches on the same route. Search in a private/incognito window to see the lowest fares.

Tip 02

Be Flexible on Airports

Flying from a nearby airport can save hundreds. Kiwi's flexible search compares nearby airports automatically.

Tip 03

Fly Tuesday or Wednesday

Mid-week departures are consistently cheaper than weekends. Any flexibility on travel days is one of the easiest ways to save.

Tip 04

Use Kiwi for Split Tickets

Virtual interlining combines tickets from multiple airlines into one cheaper itinerary - especially on long-haul routes.

Tip 05

Insure Before You Fly

EKTA policies issue in minutes and are accepted by embassies. A fraction of the cost of one medical emergency abroad.

Tip 06

Bundle Flights + Hotels

FAQ

Cheap Flight Questions, Answered

How do I find the cheapest flights with AI?

Search flexible dates across multiple airlines at once. FareFinderAI compares Aviasales, Kiwi, and Trip.com so you see the lowest fare on your route - plus nearby airports and alternate dates that are often cheaper.

What is a hidden city fare?

A hidden city (or skiplagging) fare is a flight where your real destination is a layover city - you book a longer itinerary and get off early. It can save up to 60% on some routes, though it carries airline-policy risks. See our full guide.

Are mistake fares real?

Yes. Airlines occasionally publish pricing errors - fares 50 to 90% below normal. They get corrected fast, so the key is finding and booking them quickly.
